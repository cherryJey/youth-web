About Us

Kamirithu Polytechnic, located in Kiambu County, is a vocational training institution focusing on technical skills and artisan courses. It offers training in areas such as hospitality, plumbing, electrical engineering, motor vehicle mechanics, and ICT, often providing practical, hands-on training to students. It aims to foster skills in engineering and technology.

Our Mission

To provide world-class training that meets local and international needs and prepare competent and work-ready graduates, empowered to impact their communities and the world through innovation.
